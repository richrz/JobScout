## Core Problems Solved

### For Candidates

#### Problem 1: The Resume Paradox
**Current State**: Resumes are easily embellished, yet employers rely on them entirely. Candidates who are honest get screened out while those who exaggerate succeed.

**Meridian Solution**: Skills Passport with verified credentials. Complete standardized challenges, get AI + human verification, earn portable proof of skills. No more "claims" â€” only verified evidence.

**Impact**: Level playing field. Career changers, bootcamp grads, self-taught developers can prove ability without traditional credentials.

#### Problem 2: The Black Hole
**Current State**: 70-80% of applications receive zero response. Candidates waste hours customizing applications that employers never read.

**Meridian Solution**: Anti-Ghosting Protocol. Employers deposit $50-200 per posting. If they fail to respond within 5 business days, candidates receive half the deposit as compensation.

**Impact**: Accountability. Employers forced to respect candidates' time. Response rates increase from 20% to 80%+.

#### Problem 3: Culture Roulette
**Current State**: Job descriptions are marketing. Candidates don't know if culture is toxic until they start the job (by then it's too late).

**Meridian Solution**: Company Intelligence System. Scrapes Glassdoor, LinkedIn, GitHub, news. Generates scores: Tech Health (0-10), Work-Life Balance (0-10), Financial Stability (0-10), Team Dynamics (0-10). Real data, not marketing.

**Impact**: Informed decisions. Avoid toxic cultures. Find companies that match work style preferences.

#### Problem 4: Compensation Confusion
**Current State**: "$150k salary" means different things at Netflix (cash-heavy) vs startup (equity-heavy). Hard to compare offers.

**Meridian Solution**: Total Compensation Intelligence. Normalizes salary + equity + bonus + benefits. Adjusts for cost of living. Shows 4-year total comp with confidence intervals.

**Impact**: True apples-to-apples comparisons. Better negotiation leverage. Avoid equity illusions.

#### Problem 5: Time Sink
**Current State**: Quality job search requires 20-40 hours/week. Tailoring resumes, tracking applications, researching companies.

**Meridian Solution**: AI Automation. Resume tailoring in 30 seconds (vs 60 minutes). Application tracking (kanban board). Company research auto-generated. Career Twin proactively finds matches.

**Impact**: Job search becomes 15 min/day check-in. Sustainable, less stressful.

#### Problem 6: Keyword Matching Failures
**Current State**: Miss great opportunities because job title is "Solutions Architect" vs "Senior Engineer" even though skills align perfectly.

**Meridian Solution**: Career Twin vector matching. Semantic understanding of skills, not keyword matching. "React developer" automatically matches Vue.js, Angular, Svelte roles (with similarity scores).

**Impact**: 3-5x more relevant matches. Don't miss opportunities due to terminology.

### For Employers (Phase 4+)

#### Problem 1: Low Signal-to-Noise Ratio
**Current State**: 200 applications per role, 90% unqualified. HR overwhelmed.

**Meridian Solution**: Skills Passport candidates pre-verified. Filter by specific verified credentials (e.g., "Show me candidates with Expert-level Python + Advanced System Design").

**Impact**: 10x improvement in applicant quality. Time-to-hire cut in half.

#### Problem 2: Resume Verification Overhead
**Current State**: Can't trust resumes. Need 3-4 interview rounds to assess skills.

**Meridian Solution**: Skills Passport includes links to actual project work + AI analysis + expert verification. See code quality before scheduling interview.

**Impact**: Skip initial screening rounds. Go straight to culture fit and team interviews.

#### Problem 3: Bias in Screening
**Current State**: Unconscious bias in resume screening (name, school, employment gaps).

**Meridian Solution**: Anonymous screening. Initial matching hides name, school, photo. Match based purely on verified skills + project work.

**Impact**: More diverse candidate pipeline. Compliance with emerging pay equity laws.

## Revolutionary Search Strategies

### Strategy 1: Problem Space Search
**Instead of**: "Senior Software Engineer"  
**Search for**: "I want to use machine learning to improve climate change modeling"

The system finds companies/roles working on that specific problem, regardless of job title. Bypasses title confusion entirely.

### Strategy 2: Career Trajectory Search
**Query**: "I'm a mid-level SRE. I want to become a Principal Engineer focused on distributed databases within 5 years."

Returns roles at companies with proven history of promoting SREs to Principal, whose tech stack includes specific databases, where current Principal Engineers have similar backgrounds.

### Strategy 3: Team DNA & Culture Search
**Query**: Set sliders/preferences:
- Communication Style: Asynchronous (vs. high-bandwidth)
- Decision Making: Data-Driven (vs. vision-driven)
- Work/Life: 9-to-5 (vs. mission-critical)
- Meeting Density: <5 hours/week

Returns jobs on teams matching these preferences based on analysis of public communication patterns, commit schedules, and employee testimonials.

### Strategy 4: Semantic Tech Stack Search
**Query**: "I know React, want to learn Vue.js"

System understands adjacencies and shows Vue roles with note: "High transferability from React (85% similarity)". Also suggests learning resources for gap areas.

### Strategy 5: Ideal Day/Week Search
**Query**: "I want to spend 50% time on feature development, 30% on mentoring juniors, 20% on scoping future projects. Max 5 hours meetings/week."

Natural language parsed into structured preferences. Matches to roles/managers with that work structure based on crowdsourced data.

### Strategy 6: "Red Flags" Inverted Search
**Query**: "Exclude any company that:
- Had layoff in last year
- Glassdoor culture rating <3.8
- Uses Jira for project management
- Requires take-home >4 hours"

Pre-filters based on deal-breakers. Saves time, prevents frustration.

### Strategy 7: Impact Radius Search
**Filter by**:
- User Base: 10k-1M (growing B2B SaaS)
- User Base: >100M (major consumer tech)
- Mission: Climate Tech | Open Source | Healthcare

Align with desired impact, not just role.

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

**Risk 1: Scraping Blocks**
- **Mitigation**: Rotate IPs, respect robots.txt, use official APIs where possible (LinkedIn Talent Solutions, Indeed API), rate limiting, partner with job boards

**Risk 2: ML Model Bias**
- **Mitigation**: Fairness audits, anonymized screening, diverse training data, transparency in scoring, user feedback loops

**Risk 3: Scale (Vector Search Performance)**
- **Mitigation**: Use specialized vector DB (Pinecone/Weaviate), index optimization, caching, horizontal scaling

**Risk 4: Skills Passport Cheating**
- **Mitigation**: Sandboxed environments, plagiarism detection (compare to known solutions), human spot-checks, IP/device fingerprinting, time limits

**Risk 5: Data Freshness (Stale Jobs)**
- **Mitigation**: Real-time scraping, verify postings daily, de-dupe aggressively, mark jobs >5 days old

### Business Risks

**Risk 1: Chicken-and-Egg (Need Candidates + Employers)**
- **Mitigation**: Start candidate-first (MVP), prove value, then pitch to employers with data

**Risk 2: Employer Adoption (Why pay deposits?)**
- **Mitigation**: Show quality improvement (verified candidates), reduce time-to-hire, highlight high-response companies in search

**Risk 3: Competition (LinkedIn, Indeed react)**
- **Mitigation**: Focus on verified credentials (defensible moat), move fast, build community

**Risk 4: Regulatory (Labor laws, data privacy)**
- **Mitigation**: GDPR compliance, EEOC guidelines for fairness, legal counsel, terms of service

**Risk 5: User Churn (Candidates leave after getting job)**
- **Mitigation**: Expected! Design for it. Focus on re-engagement when they search again (avg every 2-3 years). Build Skills Passport value so they return to upskill.
