# Company-in-a-Box Plan (Founder Voice)

I am building the candidate-first OS for AI careers. The market is flooded with AI-spam resumes and recruiter incentives that do not align with the candidate. I want the opposite: credible, voice-preserving scale that helps people move fast without losing who they are.

## Assumptions (stated upfront)
- My first 90 days serve AI-curious software engineers who want to transition.
- They will pay for higher interview confidence and less editing time.
- The product already exists in progress; this is a focus reset, not a rebuild.

---

## The 15 Questions, Answered
1) Best user in first 90 days: AI-curious software engineers transitioning now. They are high intent, high capability, and feel the pain daily.
2) One outcome that makes them pay: bulk tailored resumes in their voice that cut editing time and increase interview confidence.
3) Core enemy: AI-spam and recruiter misalignment that bury real fit.
4) Sharpest wedge: only AI-relevant roles and candidate-first tools. No generic job board, no employer tools.
5) Hero feature (60 seconds): ToneClone + FitFingerprint that shows exactly how a resume matches the job, with credibility signals.
6) Paid vs free: Free = search + triage + basic tracking. Paid = bulk ToneClone, multiple voice presets, FitFingerprint, advanced filters.
7) Credible messaging: "Scale your job search, stay in your voice." No claims like "seen first."
8) AI-relevant in one sentence: A role where AI is central to responsibilities, tools, or outcomes, not just a passing mention.
9) Simplest trust signal: a FitFingerprint that highlights evidence-backed claims and warns about weak or inflated alignment.
10) Remove/postpone: interview prep, employer marketplace, community, full ATS.
11) Kill criteria: no paid conversions by day 90 or weak weekly retention (<10%).
12) Biggest risks: data rights, relevance misclassification, trust backlash, unclear positioning.
13) Pricing ladder: Free, Pro ($19), Premium ($49) with more presets and bulk capacity.
14) First 2 channels: Reddit (targeted subs) and AI career newsletters/bootcamps.
15) Winning metrics in 90 days: repeat weekly usage, resume-tailor rate, paid conversion, user-reported interview lift.

---

## 1-Page PRD (Founder Voice)
- Vision: The trusted place to find and win AI-relevant roles without losing your voice.
- Target user: AI-curious software engineers transitioning into AI.
- Value prop: credible, voice-preserving scale that beats AI spam and recruiter steering.
- Scope (MVP): AI-relevant discovery, swipe triage, ToneClone tailoring, FitFingerprint report, tracking notes.
- Non-goals: full ATS, employer tools, interview prep, community.

---

## Directional Market Analysis (Short)
- Force: AI is spreading into every role; candidates need clarity and credibility, not more volume.
- Competition: generic boards optimize for volume; niche boards lack powerful candidate tools.
- Gap: no trusted AI-only destination that preserves voice and proves fit.
- Timing: AI resume spam is rising; a candidate-first counterweight is overdue.

---

## Messaging Stack (Current Winner)
- Hero: Massively scale, unmistakably you.
- Subhead: Stop being steered. Start steering.
- Proof bullets:
  - Curated for relevance, not volume.
  - Your search, your voice, your advantage.
  - AI jobs that never make the front page.
- FOMO line: Discover roles you did not know existed.

---

## 90-Day Focus Plan
- Days 1-30: lock AI relevance, ship ToneClone first pass, ship search + triage.
- Days 31-60: add FitFingerprint and tighten credibility signals.
- Days 61-90: add paid tier and run focused distribution tests.

---

## Risks + Mitigations
- Data rights: use vendors with clear redistribution rights.
- Relevance errors: user feedback loop + conservative filters.
- Trust backlash: show evidence mapping, avoid overpromises.
- Positioning drift: keep the wedge narrow and visible on the homepage.

---

## Kill List (Cut for Now)
- Employer marketplace
- Interview prep and coaching
- Full ATS or application automation
- Community and social features
- Resume builder beyond ToneClone

---

## Company-in-a-Box Summary
- Product: AI-only careers engine + ToneClone + FitFingerprint + tracking.
- Data asset: clean, deduped AI-relevant job index.
- Distribution: Reddit and AI career newsletters first.
- Revenue: candidate subscriptions, employer tools later.
- Brand: candidate-first, credibility-first, noise-free.
